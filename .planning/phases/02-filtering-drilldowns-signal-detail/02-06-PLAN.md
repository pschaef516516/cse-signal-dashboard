    1. Confirm filter pills (7d / 30d / 90d / All) appear in the header bar, same ROW as the tab buttons (right side).

    5. Switch to Enrollment & Upsell tab. The filter should be its own state (default = All). Click "30d". E&U stat cards + charts update accordingly.
    6. Switch back to Churn tab. The Churn filter should still be "7d" (per D-03 — per-tab state).

    7. Hover any bar in the "Signals by Community" chart on the Churn tab — cursor should become a pointer. Click any bar (e.g. Reddit). A drawer slides in from the right (~480px wide) with the title like "Reddit · Churn Signals".
    8. Confirm the drawer shows signal cards each with org name, source, severity, confidence, match method, created date.
    9. Click the dimmed backdrop (anywhere outside the drawer panel). The drawer should close smoothly.
    10. With the drawer closed, confirm the page is fully interactive (Pitfall 3) — try clicking a tab or another bar.
    11. Repeat for SignalVolumeChart (click a week stack — title reads "Week of YYYY-Wnn · Churn Signals") and for SeverityChart (title "[Severity] Severity · Churn Signals").
    12. Confirm MatchRateChart, ConfidenceHistogram, PostsVsSignalsChart are NOT clickable on the Churn tab.

    **Drill-downs on E&U tab (D-05 fix from cross-AI review):**
    13. Switch to Enrollment & Upsell tab. Click a bar in "Signal Sources by Type" — drawer slides in with title "[source] · Enrollment & Upsell Signals". Confirm the drawer's signal cards are E&U-typed (signal_type = enrollment or upsell).

    **Detail view (D-08, D-09, D-10, D-11):**
    14. With any drawer open, click a signal card. The list is replaced by a detail view inside the SAME drawer.
    15. Confirm the detail view shows: a "Back to signals" button at the top, a metadata block (Org, Source, Signal Type, Severity, Confidence, Match Method, Created), then "Key Quote" with a blue-left-bordered quote block, "Summary" paragraph, and a "Suggested Action" callout in a light green card.
    16. Click "Back to signals". The list reappears.
    17. Click backdrop to close. Reopen the drawer (any bar). Confirm it shows the LIST, not the last viewed detail (Pitfall 2).

    **E&U tab enrichment (D-12, D-13):**
    18. On Enrollment & Upsell, confirm two new stat cards: "Enrollment Signals" and "Upsell Signals", each with a distinct count.
    19. Confirm "Enrollment vs Upsell Volume Over Time" stacked bar chart appears (blue + purple bars by week).
    20. Confirm "Signal Sources by Type" horizontal bar chart appears with TWO bars per source.

    **Top Orgs removal (D-14):**
    21. Confirm NO "Top Orgs" table on either tab.

    **Browse tab (D-15, D-16, D-17, D-18, D-19, D-20):**

    23. Confirm the date picker at the top shows yesterday's date in YYYY-MM-DD form (e.g. if today is 2026-04-30, picker reads "2026-04-29"). Filter pills should NOT appear on this tab.
    24. Confirm two sections render: "Signals (N)" and "Posts (N)". If yesterday had any signals, the Signals section shows rows with columns Org / Signal Type / Severity / Source / Confidence / Created.     27. Change the date picker to a date that you expect to be empty (e.g. 1900-01-01). The Signals section should show "No signals found for this date" and the Posts section should show "No posts found for this date" (D-20 — exact strings).
